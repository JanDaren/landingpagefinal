My Voyage Before Death.

AN AUTOBIOGRAPHY OF JAN DAREN C. DASALLAS
CHILDHOOD YEARS.

Jan Daren Corporal Dasallas was born on the 27th day of August 2002.1 He is the third child of Marilyn and Dante Dasallas.2 Daet, Camarines Norte is his birthplace.3 It is a simple hometown with a lot of festivals and traditions.4 He was a mischievous and stubborn kid back then.5 He always gets scolded by his parents.6 He also had gotten stitches on his forehead due to his clumsiness.7

They have a simple family with six members; his mother, his father, him, and his 3 brothers.8 His mother is the only woman in their household and she is the glue that sticks them together.9 She was a professor at the University of Camarines Norte.10 His mother was a hardworking parent that supports and take care of all of them.11 His father was strict and firm.12 He was also a professor and a co-owner of a caregiving school but due to an illness, he had to stop working.13

Daren studied elementary in Abaño Elementary Laboratory School.14 He became MTAP quizzer from grades 1 to 2.15 He also became a snare drummer in the Drum and Lyre Corps.16 His favorite hobby back then was playing and running outside.17 He also plays Wii and League of Legends with his elementary friends after classes.18 He used to have a small laboratory that he created by layering unused hollow blocks and plywood as walls.19 There, he invented things from scraps such as an x-bow made from coconut lumber.20 Daren was a simple and imaginative kid back then.21

TEENAGE YEARS.

During his teenage years, he had experience a new kind of environment.1 He enrolled to a new school having new classmates, new teachers, and new friends.2 He studied junior high school in Camarines Norte National Highschool - Annex.3 There occurred some of the best memories of his life.4 He and his friends use to play track and base, patintero, Chinese garter, and more.5 They also played pellet gun war from a small forest near their school.6 He had great and hilarious friends that make his highschool life fun.7

Up to this day, they still meet and bond together.8 In this part of his life, he discovered things that he love to do such as programming.9 His ICT teacher thought them to create website, use arduino code, and create an app.10 That’s where he knew that he wants to be a programmer.11 Daren created many games from an app called Scratch.12 He was passionate about programming. also likes music very much.13 When there is a vacant class, he and his friends play guitar and sing along with all of his classmates.14 They even have a drum set in their room made from basin and broken electric fans.15 They created a real band called the Ampersand.16 There he plays bass and his friends thought him how to use it.17

After junior high, He studied senior high school in Mabini Colleges, Inc.18 He took the STEM strand since it is related to his dream course-- Computer Science.19 During his grade 12 years, the pandemic hit.20 He spent his days inside their house having online class, gaming, and playing music instruments.21

COLLEGE YEARS.

Right before college, an awful thing happened.1 Daren’s mother got diagnosed with Stage 3C Ovarian Cancer.2 It has become the darkest and lowest point of his life.3 He did not know what to do.4 She is her mother and she’s the only one he depends on.5 He and his family had a lot of struggles during these times.6 Her mother had to undergo chemotherapy in another city because it is not available in Daet.7 Her mother was an independent and brave woman, however, cancer is not really a battle.8 There’s no win or lose in this illness, only survival, struggles, and suffering.9 On the 21st Day of September 2021, his mother passed away.10 The pain and agony she experienced have come to an end for good.11 It was a depressing day for his family and she will not be forgotten.12

He never expects to be here in Manila and study at the University of the East.13 His uncle helps him with his tuition and lets him enroll in the institution.14 Daren took the computer science course he always dreamed of, and got in.15 In 1st year, he achieved the college scholarship granted by the institution.16 Today he is still pursuing his dreams to become a better person, a well-known programmer, and to make her mother proud.17

We do not know what might happen in the future, just like we do not know where might a person go when they die.18 I always thought that living is pointless because we all are going to die someday, but turns out, it’s the bright side.19 Might as well live a life for the world to be remembered.20 We are all going to die someday!21
